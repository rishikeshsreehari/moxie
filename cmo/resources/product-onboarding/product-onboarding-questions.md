# Product onboarding questions (CMO) — reusable across all products

Owner: Moxie
Created: 2026-04-01
Purpose: When Rishi adds a new product, these questions prevent missing fundamentals (tracking, ICP, pricing, channels, assets).

How to use:
- Rishi answers quickly (rough answers are fine).
- Moxie uses answers to: set first 7-day plan, assign workers, create directory + Reddit + SEO targets, and define metrics.

---

## 0) Basics
1) Product name + one-line description?
2) URL(s): marketing site, app, docs?
3) What does the product do in 1 sentence (no buzzwords)?
4) What’s the single “job to be done” / outcome?

## 1) ICP + geography
5) Who is the buyer/user (role + company type)?
6) Primary geos (top 3) and why?
7) What is the *non-goal* ICP (who we should not target)?

## 2) Channel priority + why (first 14 days)
8) Pick 2 primary acquisition channels for the next 14 days and why.
9) What’s the fastest “distribution surface” we can hit tomorrow (directories / Reddit / partnerships / outbound)?

## 3) Pain, alternatives, and positioning
10) What pain triggers someone to search for this?
11) Top 3 alternatives they use today (tools or manual process)?
12) What’s our differentiator in 1 sentence (what we do better/cheaper/faster)?
13) What’s the strongest objection (why they won’t buy)?

## 4) Offer + pricing
14) Pricing model (free/trial/freemium/paid)?
15) Price points + what’s included?
16) “Aha moment” (what must happen in first 5 minutes for conversion)?

## 5) Activation + lifecycle
17) What is the activation event (what action = “activated”)?
18) What are the top 3 drop-off points in onboarding (guess is fine)?
19) What lifecycle touchpoints exist (emails, in-app, WhatsApp)?

## 6) Analytics + attribution (must-have)
20) What analytics is installed (Umami/GSC/others)?
21) Do we have tracked events for:
   - primary CTA click
   - signup start
   - activation event
   - purchase
22) Current baseline: uniques/day, signups/week, paid/week?

## 7) SEO readiness
23) Do we have:
   - sitemap
   - robots.txt
   - canonical URLs
   - clean URLs for key pages
   - at least 3 “money pages”
24) Top 5 keywords we want to rank for (intent-first)?

## 8) Distribution assets (what we can paste everywhere)
25) Logo (sizes), screenshots, 1 GIF/demo?
26) Directory pack ready? (tagline, short/long descriptions)
27) 3 “story posts” angles (founder narrative) we can reuse?

## 9) Compliance / risk
28) Any compliance constraints (SMS, email, WhatsApp, privacy, region rules)?
29) Any brand-risk lines we must not cross?

## 10) Ops constraints
30) What’s founder time budget per day for manual execution (10 min / 30 min / 60 min)?
31) Any dependencies waiting on third parties (app store approvals, marketplace reviews, etc.)?

---

## Output I will produce after you answer
- 7-day execution plan (2 directories/day + community comments + 1 core content piece)
- instrumentation checklist
- “single metric that matters” + weekly targets
- worker assignments (who does what)
