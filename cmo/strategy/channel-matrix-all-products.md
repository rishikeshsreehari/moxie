    35|- Replies: 10-20/day (dedicate 20-40 minutes), 5 days/week
    36|
    37|Ideal days:
    38|- Tue–Thu (best)
    39|- Mon/Fri (OK for lighter posts)
    40|- Avoid: Sunday
    41|
    42|Best times:
    43|- Window A (11:00–13:00 GST) for HQ + FormBeep
    44|- Window B (18:00–20:00 GST) for StackStats
    45|
    46|Execution packet (minimum):
    47|- Product: [HQ/FormBeep/StackStats]
    48|- Type: [value/system/failure→fix/build log/product moment]

    51|- Proof element: [screenshot / metric / artifact snippet]

    58|## 2) Product Hunt (Launch)
    59|
    60|Primary goal: coordinated spike + social proof + backlinks
    61|
    62|Cadence:
    63|- Major launch: 1 per product per meaningful release (not weekly)
    64|- Prep: 7–10 days ahead (assets + maker comments + outreach)
    65|
    66|Ideal days:
    67|- Tue / Wed / Thu (best)
    68|- Avoid: weekends; Mon (catch-up); Fri (attention drop)
    69|
    70|Best times:
    71|- Launch reset is 00:00 PT. For founder sanity, we prep everything the day prior.
    72|- Day-of: be active during Window B (18:00–20:00 GST) for US overlap.
    73|
    74|Execution packet (minimum):
    75|- Tagline (≤60 chars)
    76|- 5 image carousel + 1 short video
    77|- First comment (story + who it’s for + use-case)
    78|- 3–5 maker replies prewritten (FAQ)
    79|- Outreach list (20 people) to DM day-of
    80|- Tracking: UTM link + daily leaderboard screenshot

    84|## 3) Reddit (Founder manual only)
    85|
    86|Primary goal: high-intent conversations + referral traffic (but high ban risk)
    87|
    88|Cadence:
    89|- Comments: 3–5/week
    90|- Posts: 1/week total across all subs until stable; may increase to 2/week after building >100 comment karma if engagement is strong
    91|- Hard cooldown: do not post twice in the same subreddit within 14 days
    92|
    93|Ideal days:
    94|- Tue–Thu (best)
    95|- Sat can work for builder communities, but only with strong value posts
    96|- Avoid: Sunday for promo-like content
    97|
    98|Best times:
    99|- Window B (18:00–20:00 GST) tends to catch US morning + WEU evening overlap
   100|
   101|Hard gates (must attach to every plan):
   102|1) Subreddit rules check (links/flair/promo)
   103|2) HISTORY CROSSCHECK report for u/rishikeshshari (last posts/comments in that sub + URLs)
   104|3) 14-day cooldown check
   105|
   106|Execution packet (minimum):
   107|- Subreddit
   108|- Rules summary + allowed link policy
   109|- History report snippet + verdict (OK / Not OK)
   110|- Post title
   111|- Post body (value-first)
   112|- Link plan (none OR link in comments OR allowed in body)
   113|- Flair
   114|- Exact time (UTC + GST)
   115|- Logging instruction (update tracker)
   116|
   117|Canonical tracker:
   118|- /root/moxie/products/formbeep/outreach/reddit-posting-tracker.md
