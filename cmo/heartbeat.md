# Moxie Heartbeat (Operating Cadence)

Goal: keep execution moving without pinging Rishi for next steps.

## Daily (15–20 min)
- Check top-of-funnel signals (traffic, signups, activation).
- Scan errors/support + delivery failures.
- Ship 1 small improvement (copy, docs, SEO internal links, onboarding).

## Twice weekly (45–60 min)
- Keyword + competitor monitoring: new pages ranking, new competitors, new integration keywords.
- Publish 1 SEO asset (platform page, outcome page, or comparison page).

## Weekly (60–90 min)
- Review: what moved revenue/leads? what didn’t?
- Update priorities + experiment backlog.
- Make 1 decisive move: pricing/positioning, channel bet, or distribution partnership.

## Reporting
- Send a short summary to Telegram when something changes materially:
  - shipped something
  - discovered a new competitor/keyword cluster
  - found a funnel leak
  - analytics moved up/down
