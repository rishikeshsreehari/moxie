# FormBeep Daily Outbound Plan

Last updated: 2026-03-30
Owner: Moxie
Status: active draft

## Goal
Create a low-volume, non-spammy outbound motion that generates conversations, demos, installs, feedback, and early paid conversions.

## Daily motion
### 1. Agency outreach — 6 contacts/day
Target:
- small web agencies
- WordPress/Webflow/no-code agencies
- lead-gen website builders

Message angle:
- instant WhatsApp lead alerts for client sites
- easy install
- repeatable across clients

### 2. Lead-heavy SMB outreach — 4 contacts/day
Target:
- home services
- clinics/dentists/med spas
- real estate teams
- consultancies with visible contact forms and clear lead capture

Message angle:
- speed-to-lead
- reply faster before leads go cold
- $4.99 is trivial if they already get more than 15 real leads/month

## Channel order
1. Email first
2. X/LinkedIn DM only when clearly relevant
3. Avoid routine contact-form submissions as a standard channel

## Daily process
1. Find 10 qualified prospects
2. Personalize each message using their site/offer/stack
3. Send 10 total messages max
4. Track outcome in outbound tracker
5. Follow up once after 3-5 business days if relevant

## Outreach rules
- No mass blasting
- No fake personalization
- No generic 'check out my tool'
- Each message should reference something real about their site or workflow
- Prioritize people who clearly have forms/live lead capture

## Suggested daily split
- 6 agencies
- 4 SMBs

## Success metrics
- replies
- installs/trials
- demos/conversations
- paid conversions
