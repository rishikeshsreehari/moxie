# FormBeep vs Zapier: What's better for instant form alerts?

I've been setting up Zapier workflows for clients for years.

You know the drill: form submits to Zapier, Zapier hits the WhatsApp API, you get a notification on your phone.

It works.

But it feels like buying a cement mixer just to drill a hole in the wall.

When I was testing early versions of FormBeep, I realized that 90 percent of the people I talked to didn't need a complex automation platform. They just needed to know immediately when a potential customer reached out.

So let me break down the difference.

## Zapier is for when you need a pipeline

Zapier is amazing when a form submission is one small part of a bigger workflow:

- You get the submission
- Zapier adds the person to HubSpot
- It sends a Slack message to #new-leads
- It creates a Trello card for the sales team
- It logs the event in a Google Sheet

If you need all of that, Zapier is the right tool. Don't replace it.

But if your only goal is "I want my phone to buzz when someone fills out a form," you're building a pipeline for a bucket of water.

## FormBeep is for when you need a notification

I built FormBeep to do one thing really well: **tell you instantly.**

It doesn't do CRM updates. It doesn't update spreadsheets.

It takes the form submission, hits your phone via WhatsApp (or SMS/email), and gets out of your way.

Most of my clients use them together. FormBeep is the instant wake-up signal. Zapier does the heavy lifting downstream.

## The pricing difference matters

Zapier gets expensive fast if you have a busy site.

FormBeep is a flat $6/month.

If you have a high-volume form, paying a flat fee makes more sense than paying every time a Zap runs.

## My actual recommendation

- **Use FormBeep if:** You just want instant WhatsApp alerts. You don't want to manage Zaps. You want a flat monthly price.
- **Use Zapier if:** You need complex multi-step workflows. You want to update other apps automatically.
- **Use both if:** You want the best of both worlds. This is what I usually recommend.

## FAQ

**Can I use FormBeep with Zapier?**
Yes. FormBeep sends the notification. You can still use Zapier for everything else downstream.

**What if I'm already using Make or n8n?**
Same logic. If Zapier or Make is handling your CRM and Slack updates, great. Add FormBeep just for the instant notification so you don't miss leads when the workflow breaks.

**Is Zapier more flexible?**
Absolutely. FormBeep is a specialist. Zapier is a generalist. Use the specialist for the job it's built for.
