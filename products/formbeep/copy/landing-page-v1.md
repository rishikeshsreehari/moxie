## FAQ
### Does FormBeep require Zapier?
No. FormBeep is designed to be the simpler path for direct form alerts.

### Will it slow down my site?
No meaningful slowdown. The goal is a lightweight integration that stays out of the way.

### Does it work with WordPress and Webflow?
Yes — both are core targets, alongside Framer and custom sites.

### Can I send alerts to WhatsApp only?
Yes, and you can also use email or SMS depending on your setup.

### Is there a free tier?
Yes — enough to test the workflow before you commit.

### Is it secure?
The product should keep the setup simple and avoid exposing sensitive keys in the browser.
