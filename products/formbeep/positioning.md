# FormBeep Positioning

Status: draft
Last updated: 2026-03-30

## Current public promise
- Never miss a lead again
- Instant alerts from your forms
- Get a WhatsApp notification the moment someone submits — before they move on to a competitor

## Early positioning read
Strengths:
- Clear pain: missed leads
- Clear mechanism: WhatsApp alerts from form submissions
- Strong speed/urgency angle

Risks:
- Can read like notification plumbing instead of revenue protection
- Public integrations page understates real compatibility

## Working wedge
Primary acquisition wedge for next 30 days:
- agencies and freelance devs serving lead-driven small businesses

## Positioning note (do NOT pivot blindly)
- Hypothesis: adding **SMS notifications** can be a US-first wedge (SMS is more universal than WhatsApp in the US).
- Recommendation: treat as an expansion of the promise (SMS + WhatsApp + email fallback), not a full pivot yet.
- Next step: validate demand and pick 1-2 landing pages to test (see /root/moxie/products/formbeep/sms-keyword-demand.md).
