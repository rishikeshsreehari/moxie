# FormBeep Paid Acquisition Plan (V2) — $10 total, WhatsApp-first geos

Context (verified on site): FormBeep = “Contact Form to WhatsApp™ Notifications” (formbeep.com). Pricing starts at $4.99/mo (Starter).

This plan is designed to:
- respect an ultra-low budget ($10 total)
- target markets where WhatsApp is a default business inbox (NOT US-first)
- prioritize highest-intent traffic over volume

## 0) Reality check: what $10 can and can’t do
With $10, ads are not a growth engine; they’re a *signal-gathering probe*.
Success = learning which message/keyword/audience produces:
- CTR > 3% (search) or > 1% (social)
- landing-page click → signup rate > 5%
- at least 1 signup (ideal)

## 1) Channel choice (only one) — Google Search (high-intent) OR Meta (cheap reach)
Pick ONE to avoid scattering.

### Option A (recommended): Google Search “WhatsApp form notifications” long-tail
Why: extremely high intent; we only pay for clicks.
Risk: low search volume.

### Option B: Meta ads (Facebook/Instagram) interest targeting
Why: cheap CPM/clicks in WhatsApp-heavy geos.
Risk: lower intent; $10 may not reach conversion.

My recommendation for $10: start with Option A for intent, then move to Option B only if we see no search volume.

## 2) Geo targeting (WhatsApp-first)
Exclude US.

Tier 1 (start here):
- India
- UAE

If budget allows expansion later:
- Brazil, Mexico

## 3) Google Search campaign structure (Option A)
Budget: $10 over 5 days ($2/day) so we gather multiple dayparts.

Campaign: “FormBeep | WA form notifications | $10 test”
- Networks: Search only (disable display)
- Bid strategy: Max clicks, set CPC cap if available
- Ad schedule: 9am–11pm local time (initially)
- Locations: India + UAE + Saudi + UK
- Languages: English + (optionally) Hindi for India if ads are English (can leave English only initially)

### Ad group 1: High intent (Exact + Phrase)
Exact match keywords:
- [form submissions to whatsapp]
- [contact form whatsapp notification]
- [website form whatsapp notifications]
- [whatsapp form notification]
- [webflow form whatsapp]
- [wordpress form whatsapp notification]

Phrase match keywords:
- "send form submissions to whatsapp"
- "contact form to whatsapp"
- "get whatsapp alerts for website forms"

### Negatives (prevent waste)
- job, hiring, careers
- apk, download, mod
- whatsapp api pricing (unless we create a dedicated page later)
- bulk whatsapp, whatsapp marketing, whatsapp blast
- chatbot, bot builder (unless we decide to compete there)

## 5) Landing page / conversion notes (no-code changes)
Given $10, we do NOT redesign. But we must ensure message match:
- The ad must say “Contact Form → WhatsApp notifications” (exact H1 on homepage matches)
- Add UTM params to ads (utm_source, utm_campaign, utm_term)

Recommended micro tweak (if possible quickly): add a short geo-relevant line near top:
“Built for WhatsApp-first teams (India / MENA / EU)”

## 6) Measurement (must-do)
- Ensure signup link carries UTMs or we at least capture referrer
- Track:
  - Impressions, CTR, CPC
  - Landing sessions
  - Signups

Success thresholds:
- CTR: 4%+
- CPC: under $0.50 (India) / under $1.50 (UAE/UK)
- Signup rate from paid: 5–15% (because intent is very specific)
