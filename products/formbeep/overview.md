Status: onboarding in progress

## Goal window
- 30-day goals: 10 paying customers, more traffic, $100 MRR

## Current product summary
FormBeep sends instant WhatsApp notifications when someone submits a website form.

## Current target market
- English-speaking buyers likely to pay
- Primary ICP: small agencies building lead-gen sites/funnels for clients
- Parallel ICP: small businesses with more than 15 meaningful inbound leads/month
- Freelance developers remain a useful secondary implementer audience

## Key strategic notes
- SMS may be a viable US expansion based on direct prospect demand.
- Agencies/freelance devs are the current likely acquisition wedge because they can repeat installs across clients.
