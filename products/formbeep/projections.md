# FormBeep Revenue and Funnel Projection

Last updated: 2026-03-30
Owner: Moxie
Status: directional model

## Important note
These are not forecasts from historical data. They are operating projections to guide targets.
They assume focused execution on integration SEO, community distribution, outreach, and onboarding improvements.

## Modeling assumptions
- Qualified visit to signup intent: 10% to 15%
- Signup intent to completed signup: 25% to 40%
- Signup to activation: 40% to 60%
- Activated to paid in short term: 10% to 25%
- Average starting paid plan: mostly Starter at $4.99, with occasional Pro/Agency lift
- Near-term ARPA assumption for new paid users: about $8 to $12 blended
