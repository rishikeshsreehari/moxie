## Executive Summary

We ran 50 live SERP queries across 5 tiers covering SMS, WhatsApp, platform-specific, comparison, and informational keywords. Key findings:

- **AI Overview presence: 33/50 queries (66%)** — Google's AI Overview dominates most terms. Landing pages now need AI Overview optimization.
- **Paid ads are nearly absent (2/50)** — Only "twilio alternative for forms" and "cheapest sms form notification" triggered ads. This confirms an organic-first market with low commercial intent (low CPC bidding). Very few competitors are spending on paid search here.
- **No dedicated "form-to-notification" specialist ranks anywhere** — The SERP is consistently dominated by three groups: form builders (Google Forms, WPForms, Gravity Forms), automation platforms (Zapier, Pabbly), and WhatsApp API apps (BeepMate, Clappia, WhatsForm).
- **Reddit is ubiquitous (31/50 queries)** — TheHelpfulMarketer, DigitalInspiring, and various tutorial authors appear alongside Reddit threads. This means community-driven content outranks product pages.
- **The "beepmate vs formbeep" SERP is 90% BeepMate-owned** — Competitor brand queries are fully owned by the brand itself; no comparison sites appear.

**Verdict: GO for US SMS positioning.** The SERP weakness is confirmed at scale: no single company owns the "form-to-notification" category, and commercial bidding is nearly zero.

## 9. AI Overview Threat

**AI Overview is present in 33/50 queries.** This means Google Answers directly above organic results for most terms. Our landing pages must:
1. Answer the query in the first 100 words (definition-style)
2. Include a Q&A/FAQ section
3. Use structured data (FAQPage, HowTo)
4. Include comparison tables (e.g., "SMS vs Email for Form Notifications")
5. Be concise and authoritative to be cited by AI Overview
