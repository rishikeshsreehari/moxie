# FormBeep — ICP

## Primary ICP (paying soon)
1. **SMBs** with lead-gen/contact forms (home services, clinics, consultancies) who want instant WhatsApp alerts.
2. **Agencies + freelance devs** shipping Webflow/WordPress sites who want “done-for-you” notifications.

## Buying triggers
- Missed leads because emails go unseen.
- Need WhatsApp-first workflow.
- Want simple embed vs Zapier/Make complexity.

## Target geos / language
- Prioritize **English-speaking buyers likely to pay** (US/UK/CA/AU + global English users).

## Key objections to address
- “Will this break my forms?”
- “Is this secure / spam-safe?”
- “How fast are alerts?”
- “Can I use it with [platform]?”
