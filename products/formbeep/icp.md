# FormBeep ICP

Last updated: 2026-03-30
Owner: Moxie
Status: working decision

## Decision
Moxie will not wait for perfect certainty. FormBeep will run a dual-ICP strategy for the next 30 days.

### ICP A — Primary: small agencies
Profile:
- 2-10 person web/digital agencies
- build lead-gen sites and contact funnels for clients
- often use WordPress, Webflow, Framer, custom forms, or webhook-based setups
- want quick client wins and repeatable installs

Why this segment:
- higher LTV
- repeat installs across client accounts
- stronger fit for Pro/Business/Agency plans
- easier to target through integration pages and practitioner communities

Core angle:
- Give every client instant lead alerts on WhatsApp.
- Reduce lead-response delay without rebuilding forms or adding a heavy automation stack.

### ICP B — Parallel: lead-heavy small businesses
Profile:
- businesses already getting more than 15 meaningful leads/month
- care about fast replies and lead-response speed
- do not hesitate at $4.99/month if value is obvious

Priority verticals to test first:
1. home services
2. clinics/dentists/med spas
3. real estate teams/agents
4. legal/consulting firms

Why this segment:
- easier early Starter-plan conversion
- direct pain from missed/late responses
- simple ROI story for a low monthly price point

Core angle:
- Reply faster before leads go cold.
- Get instant WhatsApp alerts the moment someone submits your form.

## What Moxie is deciding without founder input
- Initial vertical priority for SMB testing: home services first
- Initial acquisition wedge for higher leverage: agencies first
- Local business owners remain a parallel track, not a replacement track

## 30-day implication
Content/copy/channels should be split across two motions:
- agency/installers: repeatability, client value, multi-site convenience
- SMB owners: speed-to-lead, missed revenue prevention, simplicity
