# FormBeep — 7-Day Growth Plan (Quantified)

Window: next 7 days
Goal: create repeatable acquisition + conversion system, measured by leading indicators and a few hard outcomes.

Important: SEO ranking movement in 7 days is limited; we will optimize for *indexing + impressions + long-tail entry rankings* while paid + distribution drive near-term signups.

## North-star outcomes (7 days)
1) New activated accounts: 10–25
2) New paid accounts: 0–3 (goal: 1)
3) New qualified leads (inquiries / replies / DMs): 15–40

## Traffic targets (7 days)
Baseline (last 7d): ~102 pageviews, ~75 visitors.
Targets:
- Pageviews: 180–350
- Visitors: 120–250
- Top referrers: Reddit + Google + “AI referrers” (ChatGPT) should grow.

## Conversion targets (7 days)
- Signup clicks (sum of Signup-* events): 15–40
- Signup conversion rate (visitor→signup): 3%–8% (requires landing improvements)
- Activation rate (signup→first successful test submission): 30%–50%

## Paid acquisition (Nova) — deliverables + performance targets
Deliverables by Day 2:
- Google Search campaign plan: 10–20 keywords, 3–6 ad groups, 3 ads/adgroup, negatives list
- Meta campaign plan: 2 audiences, 6 ads, 3 creative angles
- Reddit ads plan: 5 subreddits, 3 ad variants, comment strategy

Performance targets by Day 7 (if we spend):
- Spend: $100–$300 total
- Cost per signup: <= $3–$8
- Signups attributable to paid: 8–25
- Paid conversions attributable: 0–2

## Content / SEO (Astra + Rumi + Iris + Kiro)
Deliverables by Day 7 (dev-ready drafts):
- Blog post drafts: 4 (ready for publish)
- Programmatic pages / tools: 2 improvements or new pages suggested (if relevant)
- Keyword set: 30 target keywords with intent + page mapping + internal links

SEO performance targets by Day 7:
- New pages indexed: 4+
- Search impressions (GSC): +20% from baseline (or first measurable impressions if baseline is near-zero)
- Long-tail rankings: 5+ keywords entering top-50 (not top-10 yet)

## Reddit / Community (Ember)
Deliverables by Day 7:
- 6 posts drafted (3 informational, 2 case-study-style, 1 comparison)
- 20 high-signal comments (non-promotional) from founder account

Performance targets:
- 2 posts published (minimum), 4 published (stretch)
- 30–120 visits from Reddit
- 3–10 qualified DMs/replies

## Directories (Jax)
Deliverables by Day 7:
- Directory master list: 40+
- Submissions: 10 submitted (minimum), 20 submitted (stretch)

Performance targets:
- 20–80 visits from directory listings over subsequent weeks (lagging)

## Partnerships / BD (Pax)
Deliverables by Day 7:
- 30 targets identified
- Outreach sequence: 3 templates
- Outreach prepared: 20 sends queued (if sending is available) OR contact sheet ready

Performance targets:
- 5 replies
- 2 calls / deep conversations

## Lifecycle / activation (Luna)
Deliverables by Day 7:
- Email set: Day 0/1/3/7/14 onboarding + activation
- Trigger spec: what event fires each email

Performance targets (if wired):
- Activation rate: +10–20% relative

## Cold email / outbound (new)
We will build outbound as a *package* unless sending infra is available.
Deliverables by Day 7:
- ICP list: Agencies + freelance devs + SMB verticals
- Prospect list: 150 leads (name/company/site/email where available)
- 3-step sequence + 5 subject lines

Performance targets (if sending is available):
- 50 sends/day for 3 days (150 total)
- Open rate: 35%+
- Reply rate: 3%+
- Qualified replies: 3+

## Instrumentation targets (Mira)
By Day 3:
- Events exist for: signup_click, connect_whatsapp_started, first_test_submission_success, pricing_view
By Day 7:
- Weekly KPI summary auto-generated and stored on disk

## Daily execution cadence
- Day 1–2: Paid plan + landing/copy plan + instrumentation spec
- Day 3–4: First drafts + directory submissions start + Reddit posting begins
- Day 5–7: Iterate on what’s working, expand distribution, tighten onboarding
